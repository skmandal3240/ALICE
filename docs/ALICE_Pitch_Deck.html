ALICE

Private. Contextual. Always with you.

A macOS menu-bar AI companion that sees your screen, hears your voice, and guides you — while keeping your data private.

Built for India's knowledge economy • v1.0 live • DMG ready

The Problem

India's knowledge workers are drowning in fragmented AI

  • 50M+ professionals constantly switch between 6+ AI tools
  • Every prompt goes to US servers → DPDP Act compliance risk
  • Voice fails on Indian accents
  • No spatial awareness — "click the blue button" instead of pointing
  • Context is lost the moment you switch tabs

Current AI tools were not built for India.

The Opportunity

India is building its AI sovereignty moment right now.

  • ₹10,000 Cr IndiaAI Mission
  • DPDP Act + data localization mandates
  • 10M+ macOS users in India, growing 25% YoY
  • High distrust of foreign clouds among enterprises & government

Whoever owns the private desktop AI layer wins the next decade in India.

Solution — ALICE

The floating AI orb that lives next to your cursor.

  • Menu-bar only (zero distraction)
  • Orb follows cursor across all monitors
  • Push-to-talk voice (Ctrl+Option) — strong Indian accent support
  • Sees your entire multi-monitor screen
  • Streams responses + natural voice
  • Points at actual UI elements
  • Zero API keys in the app (secure gateway)
